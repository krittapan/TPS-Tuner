Privacy Policy

TPS TURNER · Effective date: 1 May 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Zenlabth (“we”, “us”) handles information when you use the mobile application TPS TURNER (“App”) on iOS.

2. Information the App processes

The App helps you configure and interact with supported hardware using Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE). Depending on your actions, the App may process:

  • Device communication data exchanged over BLE between your iPhone or iPad and your hardware (for example, configuration values and status information while you use the App).
  • Files you choose using the system file or photo picker (for example, when you import or export configuration data), processed on your device as part of app functionality.

3. Personal data collection

We do not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location through the App for our own purposes.

If you contact us for support, we may receive information you voluntarily provide (for example, your email address and message content) solely to respond to your request.

4. Third-party services

The App relies on standard platform services provided by Apple (for example, permission prompts and system pickers). We do not sell your personal information.

If we integrate third-party analytics or crash reporting in the future, we will update this policy and the App Store privacy disclosures accordingly.

5. Data storage and retention

Configuration data you import or export is stored and managed on your device (or locations you choose through iOS), unless you separately choose to share it outside the App. The App does not require a user account as described in this policy.

6. Security

We follow reasonable practices for a local companion utility. No method of electronic transmission is completely secure.

7. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age required in your region, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. International users

If you use the App outside Thailand, information may be processed where you use the device and where you contact us.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will change when we do.
